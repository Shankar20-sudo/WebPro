About Pushti Milk Co.

For generations, Pushti Milk Co. has been a trusted name in premium dairy products. Located in the heart of Banasthali, Rajasthan, our family-owned farm has been dedicated to producing the finest quality milk and dairy products.

We believe in traditional farming practices combined with modern hygiene standards. Our cows are raised in a stress-free environment with natural feed, ensuring the purest and most nutritious milk for your family.
